ABOUT THE BOOK

"Abandoned House on the Lucknow street" is one of the best horror tale that will keep you on the corner of your seat. Gaurang, a YouTuber with a craze of old things slips upon an abandoned house in Lucknow with a dark history. Despite the warnings of locals, he buys the house and starts making daily vlogs. But as paranormal activities begin to occur, Gaurang and his friend Harsh dive deep into the history of the house, uncovering a horrifying past of murder and black magic.As they investigate further, they find themselves trapped in the house, facing their worst nightmares and struggling to survive against the sinister that haunt the house. In this heart-pumping tale, Gaurang and Harsh must fight for their lives and confront the horrors of the abandoned house before it's too late. Will they make it out alive, or will they surrender to the shadows that hide within the house? Get ready for a terrifying journey that will leave you trembling until the very end
